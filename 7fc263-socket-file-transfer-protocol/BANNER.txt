╠═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                       ║
║  Protocol: TCP (SOCK_STREAM)                                         ║
║  Buffer Size: 4KB chunks                                             ║
║  Checksum: MD5                                                       ║
║  Default Port: 9999                                                  ║
║  Max Retries: 5 attempts                                             ║
║  Backoff: 1s → 2s → 4s → 8s → 16s → 32s                             ║
